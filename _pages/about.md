I am Xiaosi Zhang. I received my Ph.D. in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science from [Vanderbilt University](https://www.vanderbilt.edu/) in 2023, where I was supported by the *Hamilton Fellowship*. Prior to that, I obtained my B.Sc. in Materials Forming and Control Engineering from [Huazhong University of Science and Technology](https://www.hust.edu.cn/) in 2018.

## Research Interests

My research lies at the intersection of nanomaterials, bioelectronics, and optoelectronics. Specific areas include:

- **Graphene-based bioelectronics** -- transparent microelectrode arrays for neural and retinal interfacing
- **Microfluidic devices** -- perforated microelectrode arrays for electrophysiological studies
- **Laser-material interactions** -- in situ monitoring of graphene properties under laser processing, vapor plume dynamics in laser welding
