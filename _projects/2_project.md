Investigated the electrical and optoelectronic properties of suspended graphene ribbons during laser-induced morphological changes using in situ monitoring techniques. This research provides insights into how laser processing can be used to tune graphene properties.

**Key publication:**
- X. Zhang, T.S. Walmsley, Y.Q. Xu, "In situ monitoring of electrical and optoelectronic properties of suspended graphene ribbons during laser-induced morphological changes," *Nanoscale Advances*, 2020.
