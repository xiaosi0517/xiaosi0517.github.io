**Q: Which candidate should I contact first?**

**Q: How long until I hear back?**  
A: US professors (Sarah Chen): 3-7 days. Chinese professors: 5-10 days. Some may take 2-3 weeks.

**Q: What if I don't hear back?**  
A: Follow-up after 2 weeks (gentle reminder). Try Tier-2 candidates after 1 week if no responses.

**Q: Should I personalize or use template?**  
A: Personalize heavily. Mention specific recent paper by name. Good personalization: 70% success. Bad template: 10% success.

**Q: What if they say no?**  
A: Ask why (usually too busy, not the right fit, already have collaborators). Move to next candidate.

**Q: How many collaborations realistic?**  
A: From 7 Tier-1 candidates: expect 1-2 active collaborations. From 22 total: expect 1-3 over 2-3 months.

**Q: Should I reach out to Category B if Category A works?**  
A: If you get 1-2 collaborations from Category A, probably stop. Quality > quantity. But if all A say no, definitely try B.

**Q: Is Category C worth contacting?**  
A: Only if A and B don't work out. Category C has lower success probability but not impossible.
